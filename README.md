# vibe-coding-bench

`creative-gen` is a content-generation service running in production. Its output
has been **degrading**, and a relaunch is coming up. Your job: figure out what's
going wrong, fix what you can, and ship the relaunch — **without making anything
worse**.

This is a timeboxed working session (your interviewer will tell you how long).
**It is not meant to be finished** — there is more here than anyone completes in
the time. We care about how you work, not how much you cross off.

## Run it

```bash
docker compose up --build
```

Frontend: http://localhost:8080

## What to submit

1. **A PR / diff** with your changes.
2. **A short writeup** (≈1 page): what you found, your hypotheses and how you
   verified them, what you changed, and what you'd do next with more time.
3. **A screen recording** of your session.

## Rules

- **AI coding tools (Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot, …) are allowed and
  encouraged.** Using them well is part of what we're evaluating.
- Work on your own.
- Please don't share or publish this problem or your solution.

## What we look for

Evidence-based diagnosis, good judgment about where to spend your time, how
effectively you direct AI agents, and clear communication — not the raw number
of lines changed.
